Common Questions

Remodels FAQ

Can you move plumbing for a kitchen or bathroom remodel?

Yes. We handle complete pipe relocation — from moving supply and drain lines to roughing in new fixture locations. We coordinate with your GC or contractor to keep the project on schedule.

Do you pull permits for remodel plumbing work?

Yes. As a Licensed Master Plumber, we pull all required permits. Permitted work protects your home's value and ensures inspections are done correctly — we handle that process for you.

What's included in a plumbing rough-in?

Rough-in includes running new supply and drain lines to the locations specified by your design plan, capping off old lines, and installing blocking before walls are closed. We coordinate timing with your GC.
