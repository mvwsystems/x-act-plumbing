Common Questions

Water Heater FAQ

Should I repair or replace my water heater?

If your water heater is under 8 years old and the repair cost is less than half of replacement, repair is usually right. If it's over 10 years old or has recurring issues, replacement is typically more cost-effective. We'll give you an honest recommendation.

How long does installation take?

A standard tank water heater replacement takes 2–3 hours. Tankless installations vary based on gas line or electrical needs, but most complete the same day.

Do you install tankless water heaters?

Yes. We install both tank and tankless units. Tankless provides on-demand hot water and is more energy-efficient long-term. We'll help you determine which fits your home and budget.
