// What's Included

Water Heater
Services We Offer

// 01

Tank Replacement

When your tank is past its prime — typically 10–15 years — replacement is more cost-effective than repeated repairs. We remove the old unit, install a new one, and have you back in hot water within hours.

// 02

Tankless Installation

Tankless water heaters deliver on-demand hot water and use significantly less energy than tank systems. We assess your home's capacity, recommend the right unit, and handle the full installation including gas line or electrical work as needed.

// 03

Emergency Repair

A leaking or completely failed water heater can't wait. We carry common replacement units and parts, enabling us to restore your hot water in a single visit whenever possible.

// 04

Sediment Flush & Maintenance

Sediment buildup reduces efficiency and shortens the life of your water heater. Annual flushing keeps your unit running at peak performance and can add years to its lifespan.

// 05

Efficiency Upgrades

Upgrading to a high-efficiency water heater can significantly lower your energy bills. We help you evaluate energy factor ratings and select the most cost-effective unit for your household or business.

// 06

Repair vs. Replace Consultation

Not sure if your water heater needs repair or replacement? We give you a straight, honest answer — not the option that's most profitable for us. If a simple repair makes sense, we'll tell you so.

// FAQ

Common
Questions

Should I repair or replace my water heater?

If the unit is over 10 years old or the repair cost exceeds 50% of replacement cost, replacement is usually the smarter investment. We'll give you an honest assessment either way.

How long does a water heater installation take?

Most standard tank replacements take 2–3 hours. Tankless installations may take 4–6 hours depending on the existing setup.

Do you install tankless water heaters?

Yes. We install both tank and tankless systems and can help you choose the right option based on your household size and hot water usage.
