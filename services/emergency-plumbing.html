// What's Included

Emergency Services
We Cover

// 01

Burst Pipe Response

A burst pipe can dump hundreds of gallons into your walls and floors within minutes. We locate and repair the break fast, then assess for downstream damage to protect your property.

// 02

Slab Leak Detection & Repair

Active slab leaks cause structural damage if left unaddressed. We use non-invasive acoustic detection to pinpoint the exact location before any concrete is cut, minimizing disruption.

// 03

Sewage Backup

Sewage backups are a health hazard and require immediate professional response. We clear the blockage, inspect the line for root intrusion or collapse, and restore safe drainage.

// 04

Water Heater Failure

A failed water heater — whether leaking, not heating, or completely out — is a same-day priority. We carry common replacement units and can often restore hot water in a single visit.

// 05

Clog & Drain Emergencies

Severe clogs that back up multiple fixtures or prevent toilet use need professional hydro-jetting or camera inspection — not a bottle of drain cleaner. We clear it completely.

// 06

Main Water Line Issues

Loss of water pressure or a wet yard may indicate a main line break. We assess the problem, shut off the source, and execute repairs to restore full service to your home or business.

// FAQ

Common
Questions

How fast can you respond to a plumbing emergency in Red Oak?

We aim to respond within 1–2 hours for emergency calls in Red Oak and the surrounding area. Call (469) 505-8362 any time — we're available 24/7.

What counts as a plumbing emergency?

Burst pipes, active slab leaks, sewage backups, sudden water heater failure, and any situation causing immediate water damage qualify as emergencies.

Should I shut off my water before you arrive?

Yes, if it's safe to do so. Locate your main shutoff valve and turn it off to minimize damage while we're on the way.
