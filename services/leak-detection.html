Frequently Asked Questions

How do you detect a slab leak without tearing up my floor?

We use electronic leak detection equipment and acoustic listening devices to pinpoint the leak location before any concrete is cut — minimizing disruption to your home.

What are the signs of a slab leak?

Hot spots on the floor, the sound of running water when nothing is on, unexplained increases in your water bill, and cracks in your foundation or flooring are common indicators.

Can a slab leak be repaired without replacing all the pipes?

Often yes. Depending on the location and severity, we may be able to do a targeted repair. In some cases, a full re-route is the more cost-effective long-term solution — we'll explain your options honestly.
