Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take on large commercial plumbing projects?

Yes. We work on commercial builds of varying sizes — from small retail and office buildouts to larger multi-unit and light industrial projects. We're experienced working directly with general contractors and project managers to stay on schedule.

What is a plumbing rough-in on a new build?

Rough-in is the phase where all supply lines, drain lines, and vent piping are run inside the walls and floors before drywall is installed. This sets the exact location for every future fixture. It's inspected before walls close, so accuracy at this stage is critical.

Can you work directly with our general contractor?

Absolutely. We coordinate directly with GCs on scheduling, inspections, and scope. As a Licensed Master Plumber, we pull all required permits and communicate clearly at each phase so the project stays on track.
