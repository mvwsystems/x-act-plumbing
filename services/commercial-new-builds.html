Commercial & New Build FAQ

Common Questions

Do you take large commercial plumbing projects?

Yes — we handle commercial projects of all sizes, from small tenant build-outs to full multi-family and light-commercial new construction. We work directly with general contractors, coordinate scheduling around the build timeline, and pull all required permits.

What is a plumbing rough-in?

A plumbing rough-in is the stage where all supply, drain, and vent lines are run through walls and floors before drywall or concrete is poured. The rough-in must pass a city inspection before walls close. X-Act handles the full rough-in scope and coordinates with the inspector so your build timeline stays on track.

Can you work with our general contractor?

Absolutely. We are GC-ready — we pull all necessary permits, show up to scheduled inspections, and communicate directly with your project manager. Our goal is zero plumbing delays on your job site.
