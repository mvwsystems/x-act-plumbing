Commercial Construction

Commercial & Residential
New Builds

At X-Act Plumbing, we specialize in providing reliable plumbing solutions for both commercial and residential new construction projects. Whether you're building a strip mall, office complex, or custom home, we deliver code-compliant systems designed for lasting performance.

Our rough-in work lays the foundation right — primary pipes and essential components installed before the finishing stage. Our top-out work completes the installations after groundwork is done, ensuring everything integrates cleanly with your finish-out team.

We handle all city permit processes from application through final inspection, making your project hassle-free from start to finish.
