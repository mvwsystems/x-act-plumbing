Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer emergency plumbing in Red Oak TX?

Yes. X-Act Plumbing provides 24/7 emergency plumbing in Red Oak, Waxahachie, and surrounding Ellis County. Burst pipes, slab leaks, water heater failure — call (469) 505-8362 and we respond fast.

Is X-Act Plumbing licensed and insured in Texas?

Yes. X-Act Plumbing LLC is fully licensed and insured in Texas. Daniel Clayburn holds a Master Plumber license and personally oversees all work.

What areas does X-Act Plumbing serve?

X-Act Plumbing serves Red Oak (75154), Waxahachie (75165), Ferris (75125), Kaufman (75152), and the greater Ellis County and DFW area.

Can X-Act Plumbing detect and repair slab leaks?

Yes. Slab leak detection and repair is a specialty. We use non-invasive diagnostic tools to pinpoint leaks before any concrete work begins, minimizing disruption to your home or business.

How long has X-Act Plumbing been in business?

X-Act Plumbing was founded by Daniel Clayburn, a Red Oak native with over 14 years of plumbing experience serving Ellis County and the greater DFW area.
