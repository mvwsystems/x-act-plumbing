Meet the Founder

Daniel Clayburn
Master Plumber

Born and raised in Red Oak, TX, Daniel discovered his passion for plumbing at 18 when he began as an apprentice. He didn't just learn the trade — he devoted himself to mastering every layer of it.

Over 14 years, he built his expertise through roles as journeyman, superintendent, and project manager on both residential and large-scale commercial projects — before earning his Master Plumber license.

Driven by hard work, integrity, and a deep commitment to community, Daniel founded X-Act Plumbing with a clear purpose: to build a family-owned business that treats every client the way he'd want his own family treated.
